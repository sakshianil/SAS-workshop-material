# Module 10 — Independent QC and Delivery

**Time:** 75 minutes  
**Outputs:** QC datasets, `PROC COMPARE` results, clean-log checklist

Reproduce key datasets with alternate logic, compare production and QC
results, inspect logs, and inventory delivery files. Learn what Pinnacle 21 and
`define.xml` contribute—and what they do not replace.

**Workplace connection:** regulated work relies on traceable review evidence,
not merely a program that ran once without a red error.
